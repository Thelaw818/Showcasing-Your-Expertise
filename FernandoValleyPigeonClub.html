Fernando Valley Pigeon Club

The Fernando Valley Pigeon Club (FVC) has been a cornerstone of the pigeon racing community since its founding in 1938. Located in Sun Valley, California, the club has grown into a hub for enthusiasts who share a passion for the sport. Known for its dedication to preserving the traditions of pigeon racing, the FVC has made significant contributions to the sport, fostering camaraderie and competition among its members.
