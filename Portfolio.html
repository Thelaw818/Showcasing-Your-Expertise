Placed in the Top 10 among over 500 competitors in a challenging 400-mile race, demonstrating the skills and resilience of my pigeons.

Project 2: Champion Bird - "Sky Ace"

Achieved 1st place in the 2023 Regional Championship with my champion bird, "Sky Ace," showcasing exceptional breeding and training methods.

Actively involved in mentoring new racers and organizing events, fostering a thriving community within the Fernando Valley Racing Pigeon Club.
